FAQ

Is Animal Company safe for children?

Animal Company is rated ESRB Teen (13+), meaning it's designed for teenagers and does not include graphic violence, sexual content, or harmful material. The game focuses on spooky adventures, social play, and creativity, making it a safe and fun environment for young players.

Is Animal Company a free-to-play VR game?

Yes, Animal Company is a free VR game. Download and play for free.

Which VR headset should I buy to play?

Animal Company is currently available on Meta Quest 2, Meta Quest 3S, Meta Quest 3, and Meta Quest Pro.

Can I play with friends online?

Yes! Animal Company is more fun with friends and you can always find new friends in the game or on our Discord server.

Does Animal Company get regular updates?

Animal Company releases new game updates every Tuesday! You can find new announcements on our Discord server or check our videos on our YouTube channel.
